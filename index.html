Origami

What is origami?

Origami is the art of folding pieces of paper (traditionally square) to make pieces of art. It is an ancient Japenese art form that has been done for many years.
There are many different techniques that can be used to create origami. For example, one can just fold a normal sheet of paper, or they use a technique called wet folding.

Glossary

Wet folding: To make a piece of paper wet for ease of shaping

Examples

Some examples of origami include animals, tesellations (repeating patterns along multiple axes), humans, and many others.
